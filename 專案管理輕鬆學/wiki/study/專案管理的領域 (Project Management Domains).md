# 專案管理的領域 (Project Management Domains)

PMP 2026 考試重新定義了知識領域的框架，從原來的 10 個知識領域（Knowledge Areas）改為 8 個 Performance Domains。這是 2026 考試的重大改變。

## 📖 原文內容

### PMP 2026 新框架：8 個 Performance Domains

| # | Performance Domain | 說明 | 占比 |
|---|------------------|------|------|
| 1 | **People Domain** | 人員和領導力相關 | 33% |
| 2 | **Process Domain** | 流程和方法相關 | 41% |
| 3 | **Business Environment Domain** | 組織和商業相關 | 26% |

### People Domain（人員領域）- 33%

**包含 8 個 Tasks：**
1. Build and manage the project team
2. Manage stakeholder engagement and communication
3. Manage conflict and implement problem-solving techniques
4. Lead and develop others
5. Manage your own performance
6. Establish and maintain team working agreements
7. Support and recognize team members
8. Foster positive team dynamics

### Process Domain（流程領域）- 41%

**包含 10 個 Tasks：**
1. Plan scope, schedule, budget, and resources
2. Manage project work
3. Monitor and control project work
4. Execute quality assurance
5. Execute and control procurement
6. Create and manage project deliverables
7. Define, manage, and communicate project changes
8. Plan and manage project risks
9. Assess and manage communications
10. Execute and manage project activities

### Business Environment Domain（商業環境領域）- 26%

**包含 8 個 Tasks：**
1. Manage project or product compliance with organizational standards
2. Evaluate and deliver project value
3. Support organizational change
4. Navigate and influence the organizational environment
5. Plan and manage project/product governance
6. Manage project and product interfaces
7. Manage project and product interfaces
8. Ensure project sustainability

### 新的 2026 強調

```
人員（33%）    影響：衝突管理、領導力、團隊協作
流程（41%）    影響：EVM、合約、變更控制
商業（26%）    影響：治理、風險、合規、變革、可持續性
                新增：AI、ESG、地緣政治
```

### 舊框架（PMBOK 5th-6th）vs 新框架（2026）

**舊 10 知識領域：**
1. Integration
2. Scope
3. Schedule
4. Cost
5. Quality
6. Resource
7. Communications
8. Risk
9. Procurement
10. Stakeholder

**新的對應關係：**
- Integration → Process Domain
- Scope, Schedule, Cost → Process Domain
- Quality → Process Domain
- Resource, Communications → People Domain
- Risk → Process + Business Domain
- Procurement → Process Domain
- Stakeholder → People Domain

---

## 🎯 重點摘要

- **2026 重大改變**：從 10 知識領域改為 8 個 Performance Domains。

- **重新分配比例**：People 從小角色變成 33% 的大頭（最多考試內容）。

- **新增內容**：AI、可持續性、地緣政治、治理。

- **三層框架**：人員、流程、商業環境涵蓋所有方面。

- **Business Domain 大幅增加**：從原來的邊邊角角變成 26%。
