### Scrum 與 XP 術語對照

- **角色與職責對照**
    - Product Owner (Scrum) $\rightarrow$ Customer (XP)
        - 擔任專案的業務代表 (Business representative)
    - ScrumMaster (Scrum) $\rightarrow$ Coach (XP)
        - 扮演敏捷專案經理 (Agile project manager) 的角色
    - Development Team (Scrum) $\rightarrow$ Team (XP)
        - 由授權的跨職能團隊 (Empowered Cross-Functional team) 組成
- **會議與流程對照**
    - Retrospective (Scrum) $\rightarrow$ Reflection (XP)
        - 屬於「經驗教訓」(Lessons learned) 風格的會議

| Scrum | Extreme Programming (XP) | Definition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sprint | Iteration | Fixed-length period of time (timebox) |
| Release | Small Release | Release to production |
| Sprint/Release Planning | Planning Game | Agile planning meetings |
| Product Owner | Customer | Business representative to project |
| Retrospective | Reflection | "Lessons learned"-style meeting |
| ScrumMaster | Coach | Agile project manager |
| Development Team | Team | Empowered Cross-Functional team |
| Daily Scrum | Daily Standup | Brief daily status meeting |

- **會議與流程對照（續）**
    - Daily Scrum (Scrum) $ightarrow$ Daily Standup (XP)
        - 每日 15 分鐘的站立會議，用來回答三個核心問題
- **術語使用的靈活性**
    - 在敏捷開發的討論中，術語有時會交替使用
        - 例如：Sprint 與 Iteration 可能互換
        - 例如：Product Owner 與 Customer 可能互換
